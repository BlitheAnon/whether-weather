Sea Power

Disco Elysium 2CD

£3.00

Release Date: Aug 9, 2021

Discs: 2

28 tracks recorded specifically for the game, now available on a double CD in a triple panel gatefold sleeve featuring images from the game.

A digital version is available via the BSP Bandcamp site which includes two extra tracks from the Final Cut edition of the game.

The game has won dozens of international awards for best game, artwork, story, narration, best new game, indie game, best debut, best innovation etc and most importantly the BAFTA best soundtrack!
Having sold over one million copies, the game is now to be released on the big platforms starting with Playstation and Stadia followed by Nintendo and X-Box

TIME magazine -  Best of the decade
"Released just months before the start of the 2020s, Disco Elysium is the perfect punctuation mark on a decade of excellent games. It’s a video game that’s steeped in the past but looking towards the future. It’s the kind of video game that only comes at the turn of the decade, that special time when something ends and something new is born."
"Disco Elysium is proof of what video game fans have known for years: that the medium has special strengths and can tell unique stories you won’t see on TV or read in a book. It’s a game that highlights how all video games are art.
